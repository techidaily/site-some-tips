## Expert Tips for Deleting Internet Trackers & Cookies in Google's Chrome Browser

Cookies are allowed by default. If you visit a website, the website may automatically save cookies on your computer. Cookies take up space. So you may want to clear them. Hear you will learn how to clear the cookies in Chrome.  
   
1\. Open Chrome Browser.  
  
2\. Right-click on the “**Customize and Control Google Chrome**” icon in upper-right corner and click**Settings**in the drop-down menu.
