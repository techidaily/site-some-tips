## Connector (BETA) Integrates ABBYY FlexiCapture with Automation Anywhere RPA

ABBYY FlexiCapture Connector for Automation Anywhere RPA is a MetaBot that provides the integration between the FlexiCapture enterprise platform and Automation Anywhere Enterprise RPA platform to automate the processing of unstructured content—documents, images, and text—turning them into structured, actionable data.

#### Download the Connector to begin using ABBYY FlexiCapture with Automation Anywhere Enterprise RPA:

* Easy access to processing of unstructured content with instant connection to ABBYY’s intelligent capture solution – all while building a process within Automation Anywhere Enterprise RPA
* Leverage leading OCR and data capture technologies to automate document-centric processes
* Automate the classification of documents and extraction of data, turning unstructured data into structured, actionable information
* Expand RPA usage to automate content-centric processes involving documents, images, and text to support strategic digital transformation initiatives

The FlexiCapture Connector for Automation Anywhere RPA requires a license of ABBYY FlexiCapture. The Automation Anywhere Connector is currently in beta release.
