## Android Users Face a New Era as Gemini Takes Over From Google Assistant, Insights From ZDNet Analysis

![Gemini vs. Google Assistant on Pixel 8](https://www.zdnet.com/a/img/resize/6c36911ae14a8cae0d916451141e2bbd6c8518c7/2024/05/13/0d796c9d-6647-4c17-81f0-b8a8d448ddc2/dsc00479.jpg?auto=webp&width=1280)

Kerry Wan/ZDNET

In a strategic maneuver to stay in the race with OpenAI and [other AI innovators](https://www.zdnet.com/article/best-ai-chatbot/), Google has decided to integrate Gemini more deeply into its ecosystem, promising users an enhanced, more intuitive, and more efficient voice assistant experience. If you're accustomed to using Google Assistant, prepare for Gemini AI to be your new voice companion. (But you can [still use Google Assistant](https://www.zdnet.com/home-and-office/work-life/unleash-google-assistant-5-handy-tricks-you-need-to-try-today/).)

**Also: [Everything announced at Made by Google 2024: Pixel 9 Pro, Fold, Gemini, Watch 3, and more](https://www.zdnet.com/article/everything-to-expect-at-made-by-google-2024-pixel-9-pro-fold-gemini-watch-3-and-more/)** 

Gemini, powered by advanced machine learning algorithms, aims to provide unprecedented personalization and contextual understanding as a voice assistant. Its deep integration with multiple Google services, such as Gmail, YouTube, and Google Drive enables the AI to better understand user preferences and habits. 

Arguably, this is one of Gemini's primary advantages -- a seamless cross-platform operability on Android phones and tablets, providing a cohesive and consistent experience regardless of the device you're using.

Users will notice right away that Gemini's superior writing capabilities set it apart from its predecessor, Google Assistant. By offering intelligent grammar, spelling, and content suggestions, Gemini is a powerful writing companion, enabling users to craft better-quality emails, documents, and messages.

It also promises faster responsiveness and the ability to support multiple simultaneous conversations, including conducting multiple searches at once. What's more, it supports multimodal interfaces, allowing you to interact using text, voice, images, and even your camera.

Gemini also offers advanced features such as improved reasoning, planning, and multilingual support for over 40 languages. Further setting it apart from Google Assistant, Gemini is programmed with natural language understanding (NLU), which promotes improved contextual awareness when you're conversing with your bot. NLU enables AI devices to comprehend the context and connotations of human communication, such as the emotion, intent, or goal behind a statement or question.

**Also:** [**AI scientist: 'We need to think outside the large language model box'**](https://www.zdnet.com/article/ai-scientist-we-need-to-think-outside-the-large-language-model-box/)

While Gemini excels in tasks involving creative writing, coding, idea generation, in-depth research, and image generation, it reportedly comes with limitations. Some users have complained that Gemini struggles with setting reminders, playing news, and controlling smart home devices. These tasks often require the assistant to pass requests back to Google Assistant, such as voice commands triggered by saying "Hey Google" on your Google Nest Hub.

The transition from Google Assistant to Gemini AI signals Google's commitment to pushing the boundaries of AI technology and delivering cutting-edge solutions. By leveraging Gemini's capabilities, Google seeks to usher in a new era of voice-assisted AI innovation and redefine how we interact with our devices.
